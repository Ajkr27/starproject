Multi-Physics Coupling Chain
📡
EM Simulation (FDTD/FEM)
S₁₁(ω, θ, φ), A(ω), RCS(θ,φ,ω) — power dissipation density Q(r) maps
🌡
Thermal Solver (FEM)
Q(r) → ΔT(r,t) via heat equation. Substrate thermal conductivity, convection BCs, radiation cooling

Mechanical Solver
ΔT → thermal strain ε_th → stress σ(r). Curvature-induced warping, delamination risk assessment
🔄
Coupled Feedback
Deformed geometry → re-run EM. ΔT → update εr(T), tanδ(T). Iterative until convergence (<1% RCS change)

Durability Assessment
