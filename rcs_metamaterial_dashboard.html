Resonance Behaviour
LC Circuit Model
Metallic unit cells form inductive and capacitive elements. Resonant frequency depends on both inductance and capacitance within each cell. Destructive interference between electric and magnetic resonances creates near-zero reflection.
A(ω) = 1 − |S₁₁(ω)|²
Z_eff(ω₀) ≈ Z₀
R = |(Z_eff−Z₀)/(Z_eff+Z₀)|² → 0
